UI

The UI for this application is very simple...

From the home page you are able to write and update recipes. Current recipes are laid out as panels in this page

If you click on one of these panels it will take you to a recipe detail page where you will view recipe information and be able to write reviews for the recipe by clicking the 'Write Recipe' button.

You can edit a recipe or review in their respective areas by clicking the pencil icon.

You can delete a recipe or review in their respective areas by clicking the trash icon.
